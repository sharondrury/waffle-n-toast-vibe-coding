EPIC 003 — Company Services Section: Testing
=============================================

Manual Test Steps
-----------------
These steps are written for a non-developer to follow.

Step 1 — Section is visible on the homepage
  1. Open the site in a browser (run `npm run dev` from vibe-coding/).
  2. Navigate to the home page (/).
  3. Scroll down past the hero section.
  Expected: A section with a light grey background appears below the hero.
  Status: passed

Step 2 — Section container styling
  1. On the homepage, observe the section below the hero.
  Expected:
    - Background is a light translucent grey (#D9D9D9CC).
    - Top-left and top-right corners are rounded.
    - Bottom corners are square.
    - The container is centred with breathing room on all sides.
  Status: passed

Step 3 — Two-column layout
  1. On a desktop-width screen, observe the layout inside the section.
  Expected:
    - Left column is narrower and contains the heading.
    - Right column contains three service cards stacked vertically.
  Status: passed

Step 4 — Sticky heading behaviour
  1. Scroll slowly through the company services section.
  Expected:
    - The heading on the left stays in view as you scroll past the cards.
    - The heading stops scrolling before the section ends (it does not
      overlap or escape the section).
  Status: passed

Step 5 — Heading typography
  1. Observe the heading text: "What's on offer for our service".
  Expected:
    - Text is large (approx 36px / 2.25rem).
    - The word "service" has a green underline.
  Status: passed

Step 6 — Service cards content
  1. Observe the three cards in the right column.
  Expected:
    - Card 1: Monitor icon, heading "Modern website", descriptive text.
    - Card 2: Server icon, heading "Hosting and Domains", descriptive text.
    - Card 3: Pen icon, heading "Design", text ending with a bold "Get in touch." link.
  Status: passed

Step 7 — "Get in touch." link
  1. On card 3 (Design), click "Get in touch."
  Expected: Navigates to the /contact page.
  Status: passed

Step 8 — Scroll entrance animation
  1. Start at the top of the page and scroll down into the company services section.
  Expected:
    - Each card slides in from the right.
    - Cards arrive one after the other (staggered, not all at once).
    - Once in view, cards stay in place when scrolling back up.
  Status: passed

Step 9 — No console errors
  1. Open browser DevTools → Console.
  2. Reload the page and scroll through the section.
  Expected: No errors in the console.
  Status: passed

Step 10 — Lint and build
  Expected: `npm run lint` passes with 0 errors. `npm run build` succeeds.
  Status: passed
