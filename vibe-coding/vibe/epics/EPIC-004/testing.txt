EPIC 004 — FAQ Section: Testing
================================

Manual Test Steps
-----------------

Step 1 — FAQ section appears on the homepage
  1. Run `npm run dev` and navigate to the home page (/).
  2. Scroll below the Company Services section.
  Expected: A white section appears with the heading
    "Frequently asked question at Waffles and Toast Web Creatives"
    and four collapsed accordion pills below it.
  Status: passed

Step 2 — Section background and spacing
  1. Observe the FAQ section.
  Expected:
    - Background is white, full width of the browser window.
    - Vertical breathing room above and below the section (50px margin).
  Status: passed

Step 3 — Floating question mark decorations
  1. Observe the four ? characters around the accordion.
  Expected:
    - Two on the left, two on the right of the content area.
    - Each is a different colour (green-light, green-dark, right-color, bottom-color).
    - All four float with slightly different movement — no two are in sync.
    - They sit outside/behind the accordion content and do not block clicking.
  Status: passed

Step 4 — Accordion closed state
  1. Observe each pill before clicking.
  Expected:
    - Question text is left-aligned, bold, in a light green background.
    - A + icon is on the right side of each pill.
    - No answer text is visible.
  Status: passed

Step 5 — Open a pill
  1. Click on "question 1".
  Expected:
    - The answer panel smoothly expands downward (not a snap/jump).
    - The + icon changes to ×.
    - The answer panel has a slightly darker green background than the question row.
  Status: passed

Step 6 — Close a pill by clicking it again
  1. With "question 1" open, click it again.
  Expected: The answer panel smoothly collapses. The × changes back to +.
  Status: passed

Step 7 — Only one pill open at a time
  1. Open "question 1". Then click "question 2".
  Expected:
    - "question 1" closes.
    - "question 2" opens.
    - Only one pill is open at any given time.
  Status: passed

Step 8 — Animation is smooth (grid-rows technique)
  1. Open and close pills several times at different speeds.
  Expected:
    - Expand and collapse are smooth in both directions.
    - No jump to full height or instant snap.
    - No sluggish ease-out (confirms max-height approach was not used).
  Status: passed

Step 9 — No console errors
  1. Open browser DevTools → Console.
  2. Reload and interact with all four pills.
  Expected: No errors in the console.
  Status: passed

Step 10 — Lint and build
  Expected: `npm run lint` passes with 0 errors. `npm run build` succeeds.
  Status: passed
