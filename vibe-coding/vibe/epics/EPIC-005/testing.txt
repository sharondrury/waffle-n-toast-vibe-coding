EPIC 005 — Showcase Carousel & Modal: Testing
==============================================

Manual Test Steps
-----------------

Step 1 — Showcase section appears on the homepage
  1. Run `npm run dev` and navigate to the home page (/).
  2. Scroll below the FAQ section.
  Expected: A white section with a horizontal strip of scrolling cards appears.
  Status: passed

Step 2 — Cards auto-scroll right to left
  1. Observe the carousel on page load.
  Expected:
    - Cards move continuously right to left at a comfortable pace (~35s cycle).
    - The scroll loops seamlessly with no visible jump or reset.
  Status: passed

Step 3 — Hover pauses the carousel
  1. Move the cursor over the carousel.
  Expected: Cards stop moving immediately.
  2. Move the cursor off the carousel.
  Expected: Cards resume from the position they stopped at (not reset to start).
  Status: passed

Step 4 — Drag the carousel
  1. Click and hold on any card, drag left or right.
  Expected: The carousel follows the cursor with no jank.
  2. Release the mouse.
  Expected: Carousel resumes auto-scroll from the new position.
  Status: passed

Step 5 — Drag does not open modal
  1. Click and drag a card a few pixels, then release.
  Expected: Modal does NOT open. Only a clean click (no movement) opens it.
  Status: passed

Step 6 — Click a card opens the correct modal
  1. Click card 1 (first card).
  Expected: Modal opens showing heading "Guy Salmon Consultancy" and three images.
  2. Click card 3.
  Expected: Modal shows "Sharon Drury Portfolio".
  Status: passed

Step 7 — Duplicated cards map to correct modal
  1. Click the 6th visible card (the first duplicate — same as card 1).
  Expected: Modal shows "Guy Salmon Consultancy" — not wrong content or an error.
  Status: passed

Step 8 — Carousel pauses while modal is open
  1. Open a modal.
  Expected: Carousel stops scrolling in the background.
  2. Close the modal.
  Expected: Carousel resumes.
  Status: passed

Step 9 — Close modal via x button
  1. Open any modal. Click the x button (top-right).
  Expected: Modal closes, carousel resumes.
  Status: passed

Step 10 — Close modal via backdrop
  1. Open any modal. Click the dark area outside the white content box.
  Expected: Modal closes.
  Status: passed

Step 11 — Clicking inside modal does NOT close it
  1. Open a modal. Click on the heading, images, or anywhere inside the white box.
  Expected: Modal stays open. (Confirms e.stopPropagation() is working correctly.)
  Status: passed

Step 12 — Modal open/close transition
  1. Open and close the modal several times.
  Expected: Subtle fade-in and scale pop-in on open. No abrupt appearance.
  Status: passed

Step 13 — No console errors
  1. Open DevTools Console, reload, interact with carousel and modal.
  Expected: No errors.
  Status: passed

Step 14 — Lint and build
  Expected: `npm run lint` passes with 0 errors. `npm run build` succeeds.
  Status: passed

Step 15 — Carousel card images render
  1. Run `npm run dev`, navigate to homepage, scroll to Showcase section.
  Expected: Each carousel card has a visible background image (not a blank/grey box).
  Cards 1–4 show different images. Card 5 matches card 1 (image1 reused).
  Status: passed

Step 16 — Modal images render
  1. Click any carousel card to open the modal.
  Expected: Three images are visible side by side inside the modal content box.
  No broken image icons. Images correspond to the correct modal entry.
  Status: passed

Step 17 — Build produces hashed image assets
  1. Run `npm run build`. Inspect dist/assets/.
  Expected: image1-*.png, image2-*.png, image3-*.png, image4-*.png all present
  (Vite-hashed filenames confirm the asset pipeline processed them correctly).
  Status: passed
